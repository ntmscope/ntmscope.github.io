Paper of the quarter award Q4 2021 for M. genavense meta-analysis

Jul 4, 2022

The ntmscope-publication "High overall mortality of Mycobacterium genavense infections and impact of antimycobacterial therapy: Systematic review and individual patient data meta-analysis" has been awarded ESM's "Paper of the Qarter Award" for Q4 2021.

We are very thankful for the positive feedback. The prize money will be invested in future and ongoing ntmscope-projects.
