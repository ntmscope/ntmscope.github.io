M. avium complex

M. avium complex (MAC) consists of several species, the most important ones being M. avium, M. intracellulare and M. chimaera cit. They are all slow gowing mycobacteria (SGM) with different ranges of pathogenicity cit. IN the past, this complex was designated as M. avium-intracellulare complex (MAI) Cit. As several additional species have entered the complex, the term MAC is currently preferred cit.
