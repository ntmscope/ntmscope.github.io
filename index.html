This is the website of NTMscope, a research project that investigates infections caused by non-tuberculous mycobacteria (NTM). We are based at the University Hospital Frankfurt and the Research Centre Borstel, Germany.

We use a variety of epidemiological, clinical, microbiological and bioinformatical methods and aim primarily at elucidating the molecular epidemiology and resistance mechanisms in different NTM.

On this homepage, we share information regarding ongoing projects as well as the latest news. If you are willing to contribute to one of our studies, you are most welcome and can contact us via the form below.
